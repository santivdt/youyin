Yin Yoga Class

Yin yoga is the counterpart of the fast and exciting everyday life. In Yin Yoga everything goes slowly and quietly, you take time for your body and your emotions, being in the moment. Yin Yoga makes you develop flexibility on the deeper levels. The flow of energy is stimulated and blockages are resolved. Yin yoga incorporates many elements of mindfulness and has a meditative and calming effect.

Meditation Class

In this class we practice different forms of meditation, breathing and relaxation techniques. Meditation is the practice of turning your attention to a single point of reference. It can involve focusing on the breath, on the body or on a word or phrase known as a mantra. In other words, meditation means turning your attention away from distracting thoughts and focusing on the present moment.

You Yin Harp Yoga

Together with Laura Lotti we organize special Yin Yoga and Meditation classes with live harp music. Laura, an Amsterdam based harp musician, creates music for ultimate relaxation. She is looking for inner peace, warmth and beautiful feelings in anyone who listens to her music. The harp with her soft, typical sound has a very peaceful and healing effect and takes you into an even deeper relaxation.
