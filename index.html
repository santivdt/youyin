YOUYIN

You Yin is a small yoga school located in the south-west of Amsterdam, close to the Vondelpark. We provide yin yoga and meditation classes in a cosy atmosphere with small groups of people. Our goal is to create and ambience for everyone to develop themselves and just become a little bit more happy. You are welcome to join us.
“We don’t use our body to get into a pose,
we use the pose to get into our body”
